The Science of Breathwork

Breathwork is a master code to switch your nervous system states instantly. When you change your breathing, you are physically stimulating your Vagus Nerve—the longest nerve in your body that acts as a "superhighway" between your lungs and your brain.

Studies show that 60 seconds of specific breathing drops your Cortisol (the stress chemical) and instantly slows your heart rate. SAVITUH makes these proven techniques accessible.
